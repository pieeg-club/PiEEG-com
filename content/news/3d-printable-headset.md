We're excited to release our latest open-source contribution: a fully 3D-printable EEG headset design!

## Professional Quality, DIY Price

This headset design brings professional-grade electrode positioning to makers and researchers on a budget. Simply download the files, print them on any FDM 3D printer, and you'll have a custom EEG cap ready to use with any of our devices.

## Features

- **Accurate electrode positioning**: Follows international 10-20 system
- **Adjustable sizing**: Fits head circumferences from 52-62cm
- **Comfortable design**: Extended wear testing approved
- **Multiple configurations**: 8, 16, or 32 electrode variants
- **Easy assembly**: No special tools required

## Download & Print

Files available on:
- [GitHub](https://github.com/pieeg-club/ironbci_3D_EEG_Printable_Headset)

Print time: 6-12 hours depending on configuration
Material: PLA, PETG, or TPU recommended

## Assembly Guide

Complete assembly instructions with photos and videos available in our documentation. Average assembly time: 30 minutes.
