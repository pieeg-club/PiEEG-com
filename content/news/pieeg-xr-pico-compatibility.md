You asked us about PICO. So we got one, and we started testing.

**PiEEG XR** is expanding beyond Meta Quest. We're now actively testing compatibility with **PICO XR** headsets, and the early results are exactly what we hoped for: optimal EEG signal quality, tight hardware fit, and real-time facial expression decoding working seamlessly in a brand-new VR ecosystem.

## Why PICO XR?

The community made this happen. PICO XR has a large and passionate user base, especially in Europe and Asia, and many of you reached out asking whether your headset would ever be supported. We listened.

PICO XR headsets share a similar form factor to Meta Quest, which means the PiEEG XR face gasket fits cleanly and the electrode placement stays consistent. No compromises on signal quality.

## One Neural Interface. Multiple Platforms.

This is the direction we're heading: a single open neural face interface that works across the major XR platforms. Brain signals don't care which headset you're wearing, and neither should your BCI.
