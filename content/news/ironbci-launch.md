We're thrilled to announce the official launch of **IronBCI** - our most affordable and accessible brain-computer interface system yet!

## What is IronBCI?

IronBCI is a complete BCI development platform that makes neuroscience research and BCI experimentation accessible to everyone. Whether you're a student, researcher, maker, or just curious about brain-computer interfaces, IronBCI provides everything you need to get started.

## Key Features

### Affordable Excellence
- **Price**: Starting at just $299
- **Complete kit**: No hidden costs or required add-ons
- **Open-source**: Full hardware and software documentation

### Technical Specifications
- **Channels**: 8 channels (IronBCI BLE) or 32 channels (IronBCI-32 USB)
- **Resolution**: 24-bit ADC for precise measurements
- **Sample Rate**: 250 Hz (8ch BLE) / 500 Hz (32ch USB)
- **Input Noise**: < 1 µVpp
- **Compatible**: Works with standard EEG electrodes and caps

### Easy to Use
- **Plug & Play**: USB connection, no complex setup
- **Cross-Platform**: Windows, macOS, Linux support
- **Software Included**: Free analysis and visualization tools
- **Python API**: Easy integration with your projects
