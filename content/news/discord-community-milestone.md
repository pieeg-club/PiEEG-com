## A Global Community

Our members come from over 80 countries, representing:
- University research labs
- Independent researchers
- Students and educators
- Makers and hobbyists
- Clinical professionals
- Software developers

Get help from experts and fellow users with:
- Hardware setup and troubleshooting
- Software configuration
- Signal processing questions
- Project development

### Project Showcase
Share your BCI projects and get feedback:
- Research publications
- DIY builds
- Software tools
- Creative applications

### Learning Resources
Access exclusive content:
- Live workshops and Q&A sessions
- Code snippets and examples
- Research paper discussions
- Career opportunities

### Collaboration
Find collaborators for:
- Research projects
- Open-source development
- Academic papers
- Hackathons and competitions
