Happy to say we have a plan to organize a Neuroscience Hackathon this Autumn in Ottawa, Canada! 🧠 🍁 

Our goal is to make revolutions in neuroscience via low-cost BCI and open-source software. Whether you are a seasoned researcher or a passionate hobbyist, we want you there.

🛠️ We Provide:
- Hardware: PiEEG devices and electrodes.
- Gear: EEG caps for all participants.
- Software: Full access to the PiEEG-server stack.
- Environment: A dedicated space and full organization.

🤝 How to Get Involved:
We are looking for participants, partners, and collaborators!
- Universities & Research Groups: Let's bridge the gap between lab research and real-world application.
- Startups: Looking to test a new use case? This is your playground.
- Individual Experts: If you don't have a team, don't worry! We will help you form one on-site.

🏆 Certification & Prizes:
Official certification and exclusive prizes from PiEEG will be awarded to the winning teams and standout projects.

💡 Project Themes We're Thinking About:
- Signals to Systems: Architecting the flow from raw data to actionable insights.
- The Open Frontier of BMIs: Exploring the next generation of Brain-Machine Interfaces.
- Neural Data to Web Traffic: Bridging the gap between the brain and the digital world.

Are you ready to participate or collaborate? Contact us through our website or community channels to start the conversation! Let's make this the most impactful neuro-event of the year.
