Introducing **MicroBCI** - the world's smallest and most power-efficient BCI platform!

## Designed for Mobility

MicroBCI is built around the powerful STM32 microcontroller, offering a complete BCI solution in a tiny form factor perfect for wearable and mobile applications.

## Key Features

### Ultra-Compact Design
- **Size**: 50mm x 35mm
- **Weight**: Just 15 grams
- **Power**: USB or battery (3.3-5V)
- **Channels**: 4 or 8 channels available

### STM32 Power
- **MCU**: STM32F4 @ 168MHz
- **RAM**: 192KB for signal processing
- **Flash**: 1MB for firmware and data logging
- **Peripherals**: SPI, I2C, UART, USB

### On-Board Processing
- Real-time filtering
- Feature extraction
- Classification algorithms
- Data logging to SD card

## Development Features

- **Arduino IDE Compatible**: Easy programming
- **STM32Cube Support**: Professional development
- **Wireless Options**: Bluetooth and WiFi shields
- **Expansion Headers**: Add your own sensors
