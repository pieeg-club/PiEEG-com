We're excited to announce our official presence on Thingiverse! All our 3D-printable EEG hardware designs are now available in one convenient location.

## What You'll Find

Visit [PiEEG on Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/PiEEG/designs) to download:

- **EEG Headsets**: Professional-grade electrode positioning systems
- **Electrode Holders**: Custom mounts for different electrode types
- **Device Enclosures**: Protective cases for PiEEG, IronBCI, and MicroBCI
- **Accessories**: Cable management, electrode caps, and more

## Why Thingiverse?

Thingiverse makes it easy for the maker community to:
- **Browse and download** STL files instantly
- **Remix and customize** designs for your needs
- **Share your builds** with the community
- **Track updates** as we release new designs

## 100% Open Source

All our designs remain free and open source. Whether you're a researcher, student, or maker, you can print professional-quality EEG hardware at home.

## Get Started

1. Visit [thingiverse.com/PiEEG/designs](https://www.thingiverse.com/PiEEG/designs)
2. Browse our collection
3. Download the files you need
4. Print and build!

Join thousands of makers worldwide building accessible neurotechnology.
