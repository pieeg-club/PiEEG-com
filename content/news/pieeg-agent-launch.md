We've built something the neuroscience community has been missing: an AI copilot that understands both your brain signals and your questions about them.

## From Jupyter Notebooks to Natural Conversations

Working with live EEG data usually means switching between signal processing scripts, visualization tools, and statistical analysis notebooks. **PiEEG-Agent** changes that entirely—it's a natural language interface that sits between your raw neural data and your research questions.

Ask it anything:
- Query your current mental state and get instant neural metrics
- Train new pattern detectors through guided conversational workflows  
- Run statistical comparisons between different cognitive states
- Analyze cross-channel relationships and signal quality on demand

The system handles the complexity while you focus on the science.

## Built for the Real World

PiEEG-Agent isn't a demo—it's production-ready infrastructure built on battle-tested components:

**Real-time streaming** via WebSocket with Lab Streaming Layer (LSL) integration means compatibility with the entire PiEEG hardware ecosystem—from Raspberry Pi prototypes to professional-grade systems.

**Multi-LLM support** lets you choose your AI backend: Claude for nuanced analysis, OpenAI for broad compatibility, Groq for speed, or Ollama for complete local privacy.

**Research-grade ML** including regularized classifiers with proper cross-validation, not just black-box predictions. The system shows you *why* it makes each decision.

**Security by design** with gated hardware access, audit logging, and dry-run modes. Your lab equipment stays safe while you experiment.

## Open Source, Open Access

This isn't proprietary lab software locked behind institutional licenses. PiEEG-Agent is fully open source because we believe the tools for understanding the brain should belong to everyone building in this space.

One command gets you running. No complex dependency management, no Node.js installation required—just clone, configure, and start exploring your neural data through conversation.
